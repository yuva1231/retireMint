About RetireMINT

A free, open retirement planning tool built to make financial planning accessible to everyone, no sign-up, no fees.

Why RetireMINT?

Retirement planning tools are often gated behind financial advisors or complex software. RetireMINT gives you a clear, honest look at your retirement trajectory using the same math: inflation-adjusted projections, live interest rates, and a life-expectancy-aware savings target, without requiring a subscription or personal data.

How the Calculator Works

RetireMINT simulates your savings growth year-by-year during your accumulation phase (today until retirement), then calculates the nest egg you'll need to sustain your target spending throughout retirement using the Present Value of a Growing Annuity formula. This accounts for:

  • Your savings compounding at the live U.S. Treasury interest rate
  • Annual income growth raising your contributions over time
  • Inflation eroding your purchasing power during retirement
  • Your specific retirement duration (life expectancy)

Results are compared against the popular 4% withdrawal rule as an additional benchmark.
